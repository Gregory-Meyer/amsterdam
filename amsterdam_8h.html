Detailed Description

Asynchronous channels for C++17.

Channels are manipulated through their Sender/Receiver components. Each thread using a Channel should have a Sender and/or Receiver to synchronize with other threads.

Channels are implemented as singly-linked lists, representing a FIFO queue. Synchronization is achieved through a shared mutex and condition variable.
